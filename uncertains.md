## Antigua and Barbuda

### Details

Twin-island nation in the Lesser Antilles, known for resorts, beaches, and sugar history.

### Geographical clue

Northeast of Dominica, east of the Caribbean Sea, just south of Guadeloupe.

---

## Comoros

### Details

Archipelago nation in the Indian Ocean with three main islands, focusing on agriculture and tourism.

### Geographical clue

Off Mozambique's coast, between Madagascar and Tanzania, in the Mozambique Channel.

---

## Dominica

### Details

Volcanic island nation dubbed "Nature Island," rich in rainforests, wildlife, and geothermal sites.

### Geographical clue

East of Guadeloupe, north of Martinique, in the Windward Islands chain.

---

## Kiribati

### Details

Equatorial island nation spanning three archipelagos, with atolls, known for being the first to see the New Millennium.

### Geographical clue

Central Pacific, straddling the equator and International Date Line, east of Hawaii.

---

## Nauru

### Details
Phosphate-rich island republic, one of the world's smallest nations, facing economic challenges post-mining.

### Geographical clue
Micronesia, northeast of Paparal, south of Marshall Islands, in the Pacific.

---

## Palau

### Details

Archipelago nation with over 500 islands, a diving paradise in Micronesia with UNESCO marine sanctuaries.

### Geographical clue

West of the Philippines, northeast of Indonesia, in the western Pacific between Guam and New Guinea.

---

## Saint Kitts and Nevis

### Details
Twin-island nation, birthplace of West Indian cricket, known for beaches and economic diversification.

### Geographical clue
Leeward Islands, northeast of Montserrat, south of Anguilla, in the eastern Caribbean.

---

Saint Lucia

### Details
Eastern Caribbean island with pitons, rainforests, and hospitality industry, between Martinique and Saint Vincent.

### Geographical clue
Windward Islands, west of Barbados, south of Martinique, with active volcanoes.

---

## Saint Vincent and the Grenadines

### Details

Island chain nation with volcanic main island and idyllic cays, popular for yachting and agriculture.

### Geographical clue

Windward Islands, south of Saint Lucia, north of Barbados, in the southeastern Caribbean.

---

## Tonga

### Details

Polynesian kingdom with islands, coral reefs, and whale watching, often overlooked culturally.

### Geographical clue

South Pacific, east of Fiji, south of Samoa, centered on Tongatapu.

---

## Trinidad and Tobago

### Details

Twin-island republic with energy resources, Carnival festival, and diverse ethnic heritage.

### Geographical clue

Off Venezuela's coast, northeast of South America, in the Caribbean at the mouth of the Orinoco River.

---

## Tuvalu

### Details

Atoll nation in the Pacific, vulnerable to climate change, with subsistence fishing and aid-dependent economy.

### Geographical clue

Micronesia, north of Fiji, west of Kiribati, just south of the equator in the Polynesian Outliers.

---

## Vanuatu

### Details

Melanesian archipelago with active volcanoes, tribal culture, and biodiversity, 80 islands inhabited.

### Geographical clue

Southwest Pacific, west of Fiji, northeast of New Caledonia, between the Coral and Solomon Seas.
